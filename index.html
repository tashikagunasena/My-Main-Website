About Me

Who I am

I am a junior web designer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My skills also include simple photo and video editing, MS Office work, and simple graphic design, helping me approach digital content in a complete way.

My background in media studies supports my work as a digital content manager. I like organized thinking, clear communication, and neat results that still feel playful and alive.
